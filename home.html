Welcome to: Stark State's
Software Developer's Guild

Who are we?

This student club is open to all students (at Stark State College) with an interest in computer programming.

What are we?

The Software Developer's Guild (SDG) is a student group for those at any level in computer programming - from the beginner to the advanced programmer.


The goal is to better prepare students for a career in software development and bring students with an interest in software development together.


And to have FUN!

Where do we meet?

We meet on the Stark State main campus on the second floor of the C building in room C215.

When do we meet?

We meet every Tuesday at 4:00 pm and typically run until 5:00pm.
